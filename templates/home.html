Using TweetSwarm could be the best thing you've ever done, or the worst.

TweetSwarm is a tool to create auto-retweeting networks of Twitter accounts

When you create a TweetSwarm, you specify a user and a hashtag to watch. When that user tweets using that hashtag, any accounts that have joined the TweetSwarm will automatically retweet the tweet.

Click here to see existing TweetSwarms, use the form on the right to create a new TweetSwarm, or click here to find out more about TweetSwarm.
