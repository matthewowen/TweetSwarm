Using TweetNet could be the best thing you've ever done, or the worst.

TweetNet is a tool to create auto-retweeting networks of Twitter accounts

When you create a TweetNet, you specify a user and a hashtag to watch. When that user tweets using that hashtag, any accounts that have joined the TweetNet will automatically retweet the tweet.

Click here to see existing TweetNets, use the form on the right to create a new TweetNet, or click here to find out more about TweetNet.
